Tax Benefits

When buying a property with loans from specific financial institutions, tax authorities provide certain benefits and exemptions from tax payments. Section 24 of the Income Tax Act states that an investor is allowed to deduct an amount equivalent to the total interest payable on the housing loan from his/her taxable income within the same financial year. According to Section 80C of the Income Tax Act: A deduction u/s 80C (2) (xviii) is available on repayment of the principal during a financial year of up to Rs. 1,00,000/-
